Administrator sign-in

Batch import

Paste your tips — one per line works well. Add #tags inline if you like (first tag = primary, the rest secondary), or leave them out and use ✨ Suggest tags. You'll review and edit everything before it's saved.
Example: Drink water first thing #health #morning

Import tags

Add tags to the allowed list, comma-separated. They'll be available to click onto any tip.
Example: health, productivity, focus, morning

Apply a tag to tips

Type a tag (new or existing) and choose its tier, then click tips below to add it — one click each. Click an already-tagged (green) tip to remove it. Changes save instantly.

Delete Tags

Click × next to a tag to delete it. Deleting a tag also removes it from every tip it was attached to (the tips themselves are kept).

Suggest a tip

Share a piece of practical wisdom. An admin reviews suggestions before they join the collection.

Review submissions

Community-suggested tips awaiting review. Edit the wording or tags (first tag = primary), then approve to add it to the collection — or reject.
