Batch import

Paste your tips. Tag a tip with #tag; the next word after a tag starts the next tip. The first tag is primary, the rest are secondary. You'll review and edit the list before anything is saved.
Example: Drink water first thing #health #morning Write your goals #productivity

Import tags

Add tags to the allowed list, comma-separated. They'll be available to click onto any tip.
Example: health, productivity, focus, morning

Apply a tag to tips

Type a tag (new or existing) and choose its tier, then click tips below to add it — one click each. Click an already-tagged (green) tip to remove it. Changes save instantly.

Delete Tags

Click × next to a tag to delete it. Deleting a tag also removes it from every tip it was attached to (the tips themselves are kept).
